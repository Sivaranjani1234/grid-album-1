Suriya

Saravanan Sivakumar (born 23 July 1975), known by his stage name Suriya, is an Indian actor and film producer. He primarily works in Tamil cinema where he is one of the highest paid actors.

Ajith Kumar

Ajith Kumar is an Indian film actor best known by his mononym Ajith or the name his fans gave him Thala and Ultimate Star known for his work in Tamil cinema.

Vijay

Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar (born 22 June 1974) is an Indian actor Vijay has acted in over 65 films and is one of the most commercially successful actors in Tamil cinema with multiple films.

Vikram

Kennedy John Victor (born 17 April 1966), better known by his stage name Vikram, is an Indian actor who predominantly works in Tamil cinema.He is among the most decorated actors in Tamil cinema.

Dulquer Salmaan

Dulquer Salmaan (born 28 July 1983/1986) is an Indian actor, playback singer and film producer who predominantly works in Malayalam films in addition to Tamil, Telugu and Hindi films.

Kamal Haasan

Kamal Haasan (born Parthasarathy Srinivasan on 7 November 1954)[4] is an Indian actor, filmmaker, screenwriter, playback singer, television presenter, social activist and politician who works mainly in Tamil cinema.

Dhanush

Venkatesh Prabhu Kasthuri Raja (born 28 July 1983), known professionally as Dhanush, is an Indian actor, producer, director, lyricist and playback singer who primarily works in Tamil cinema.

Rajinikanth

Shivaji Rao Gaikwad (born 12 December 1950), known professionally as Rajinikanth is an Indian actor who works mainly in Tamil cinema. He has done 169 films that includes films in Tamil, Hindi, Telugu, Kannada, Bengali, and Malayalam.
